Computer Science Department Admissions


Our computer science program is designed to provide students with a strong foundation in the fundamental principles of computer science. The curriculum is regularly updated to reflect the latest industry trends and technological advancements. Renowned Faculty Our faculty members are experts in their respective fields, bringing a wealth of knowledge and experience to the classroom. They are dedicated to providing high-quality education and mentoring to help students succeed. State-of-the-Art Facilities Students have access to modern labs and facilities equipped with the latest technology, providing hands-on experience in programming, software development, and computer systems. Research Opportunities Our department actively encourages and supports student research. Engage in groundbreaking projects and collaborate with faculty members on cutting-edge research initiatives.

Admission Information
Application Process
Online Application: Complete our user-friendly online application form.
Submit Documents: Upload the required documents, including transcripts, letters of recommendation, and a personal statement.
Application Fee: Pay the non-refundable application fee through our secure payment portal.

Admission Requirements: Applicants must have taken the WASSCE and obtained an aggregate 10 with at least a B3 in Elective Mathematics.
Applicants must have obtained at least a C4 in Core Mathematics, English, Social Studies, and Integrated Science
Successful students will be admitted into the Mathematical Science Programme and be transitioned into Computer Science based on performance in the first year.
